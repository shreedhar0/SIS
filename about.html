About this software

This is the software to store a data about students and this stored data can be accessed using computers, mobile devices with the help of internet connection.
It is easy to see information of student from anywhere. Students and their parents are allowed to see their data.
It is convenient to teachers and other teaching staff members to manage and use data of particular student with help of this software.
